Poetry

Words from ice, sea, and everything in between.

25th of August 2025

*And if you see*

And if you see the sun, remember
how your smile used to warm my heart
And if you see the little things in life,
sleeping, drifting as ice floes apart

it's where the science fails, my love,
to get the uncertainties between the seen and the unseen
The clouds, the earth and all our simulations,
make our daily life seem so obscene

And if you see,
then just keep looking
cause physics in the particles are there
and are only for the ones that are looking,
the fool ones enough to dare
