Aikaterini Tavri

Sea Ice · Waves · Remote Sensing

As a scientist and educator, I am fascinated by the physical processes that shape the polar oceans – especially the dynamic marginal ice zone where sea ice meets open water. Satellite remote sensing and machine learning allow me to investigate how waves, ice, and the upper ocean interact across scales. Through the lens of microwave sensors, I work to improve our understanding of sea-ice variability and to advance the way these processes are represented in climate models.
