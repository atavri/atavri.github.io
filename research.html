Current Research

Antarctic SIC Bias Correction Using Multi-Sensor Data

I investigate how ocean waves in the Marginal Ice Zone influence passive microwave sea ice concentration (SIC) retrievals in Antarctica. My work combines Sentinel-1, Sentinel-2, and ICESat-2 data to detect wave-affected ice and quantify bias in widely used SIC datasets.

NASA-ISRO SAR Mission (NISAR) Early Adopter

I use L-band NISAR together with C-band Sentinel-1 to improve SIC retrievals during the Antarctic melt season, especially where wave-ice interactions degrade retrieval accuracy.

Arctic Langmuir Turbulence from Coupled Models

Using coupled NeXtSIM and WaveWatch III simulations, I explore potential for Langmuir turbulence under realistic wave-ice coupling. This helps understand upper ocean mixing and its representation in climate models.
